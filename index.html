Statue of Oneness

A tribute to Adi Shankaracharya & the philosophy of Advaita Vedanta

Location & Overview

Place: Omkareshwar, MP
Nearby: 80km from Indore
Height: 189ft
Weight: 100 tonnes
Inaugurated: Sept 21, 2023
By: CM Shivraj Singh Chouhan

Features & Sustainability

Material: 88% copper alloy
Lifespan: 500 years
Wind Resistant: 180 km/h
Seismic: Zone 3
Eco: Solar + Rainwater
Plants: Ayurvedic herbs

Philosophy & Cultural Importance

The Statue of Oneness symbolizes Advaita Vedanta—the timeless Indian philosophy of non-duality. Depicting Adi Shankaracharya as a 12-year-old, it inspires spiritual awakening, peace, and unity beyond boundaries.
